How to explore Gdańsk
through its churches

See how Gdańsk evolved across 800 years — through its churches, rulers, wars and major events.

🖥 On desktop
  • Scan the timeline horizontally to move through centuries
  • Each row is one church — follow its history across time
  • Enable + Global Context to overlay wars, rulers & events
  • Click any marker or bar to see details
📱 On mobile
  • Start with the list of churches
  • Tap a church to explore its timeline and events
  • Use search and filters to find specific churches
  • Enable + Global Context to add historical events
💡 Turn on Global Context to see how churches connect to wars, politics and epidemics
